# Babylon

> **Scope:** Babylon as a geopolitical, theological, and prophetic entity in Scripture  

> **Method:** Chronological ordering of biblical texts (exegetical; no commentary)  

> **Note:** This page functions as a **reference backend** for Daniel, Revelation, and related studies
